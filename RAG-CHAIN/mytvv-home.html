Henry Danger

Genre: Superhero, Comedy, Action
Creator: Dana Olsen, David M. Israel
Original Run: July 26, 2014 - March 21, 2020
Network: Nickelodeon
Number of Seasons: 5
Number of Episodes: 121

At the beginning of the show, Henry explains how his life changed forever. He mentions how Captain Man's father, an irresponsible scientist, accidentally made him indestructible. Henry wanted a simple after-school job and stumbled upon a creepy old store, which led him to becoming Captain Man's sidekick, Kid Danger.
